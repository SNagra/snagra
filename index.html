Annotate!

View project on GitHub

Annotate! is an iMessage sticker app that lets you mark up your texts and photos. Need to pinpoint and point out something important to friends & family? Use Annotate! to help you point out the details. Always use the right tools, whether they are diagrams, circles, arrows, lines, text, numbers, or symbols.
